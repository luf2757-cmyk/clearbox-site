Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-25 · Product: ClearBox · Publisher: AIGC Zhangyue

ClearBox provides on-device cleanup tools for photos, contacts, and calendar items. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed, how it is used, where it is stored, and what choices you have.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the ClearBox iOS application, in-app subscription flows, support channels, and related service pages.

2. Data We Process

2.1 Device and app runtime data

  • App version, iOS version, and model class for support diagnostics.
  • Local app state such as cleanup history, streak counters, and feature flags.
  • A locally generated app user identifier (for support and troubleshooting continuity).

2.2 Cleanup-related data (on-device)

  • Photo/video metadata and local asset identifiers used for duplicate/similar/screenshot/large-video analysis.
  • Contact metadata for duplicate-contact suggestions.
  • Calendar metadata for stale-event suggestions.

2.3 Subscription and payment

  • Subscription transaction and entitlement information is provided by Apple StoreKit.
  • ClearBox does not collect or store your card number or billing instrument details directly.

3. How We Process Data

  • Core cleanup analysis is performed on-device by default.
  • Photo/contact/calendar content is not uploaded as a prerequisite for basic cleanup features.
  • If cloud features are added in future versions, we will explicitly disclose data purpose, retention, and controls.

4. Legal Basis and Purpose

Depending on jurisdiction, processing may rely on:

  • Performance of service: enabling scan, grouping, and cleanup flows requested by the user.
  • Legitimate interests: quality assurance, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Consent: system permissions such as Photos/Contacts/Calendar access.

5. Permissions

  • Photos: required to scan and remove selected media items.
  • Contacts: required to detect potential duplicate contacts.
  • Calendar: required to detect stale calendar entries.

You can revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Revocation may limit feature availability.

6. Data Retention

  • Local operational records are stored on your device.
  • Retention duration depends on app usage, local clearing actions, uninstall behavior, and iOS system cleanup policies.
  • When supported, users can clear local records through app settings and reinstall behavior.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data sharing is limited to what is necessary for Apple billing infrastructure and core platform APIs.

8. International Transfers

Because core processing is on-device, cross-border transfer exposure is reduced. If remote services are introduced, transfer safeguards will be disclosed before rollout.

9. Security

  • Reasonable safeguards are applied to local app state and identifiers.
  • Users are responsible for device passcode, account security, and OS update hygiene.

10. Children and Minors

If you are under the age of majority in your region, use the app under guardian supervision.

11. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may have rights to:

  • Access information about processing activities.
  • Request correction or deletion where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for permission-based processing.
  • Object to specific processing or lodge complaints with regulators.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect feature changes, legal updates, or security improvements. Material changes will be reflected with an updated date and in-app notice when appropriate.
