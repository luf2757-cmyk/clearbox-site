Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-04-25 · App: ClearBox · Bundle ID: com.aigc.zhangyue.app

This Privacy Policy explains what information ClearBox processes, why it is processed, where it is stored, how long it is retained, and what controls you have.

Core Principle: ClearBox performs cleanup analysis on-device by default. Photo/contacts/calendar content is not uploaded as a prerequisite for basic cleanup features.

1. Scope

This policy applies to the ClearBox iOS app, subscription purchase flows (Apple IAP), and support channels.

2. Information We Process

2.1 Device and runtime information

  • App version, iOS version, and device model used for diagnostics and support.
  • Local app state (cleanup history, streak counters, and presentation settings).
  • A local app user identifier for support continuity and troubleshooting.

2.2 Cleanup-related metadata (on-device)

  • Photo/video metadata and local asset identifiers for duplicate/similar/screenshot/large-video flows.
  • Contact metadata for duplicate-contact suggestions.
  • Calendar metadata for stale-event suggestions.

2.3 Subscription and billing data

  • Subscription entitlements are obtained from Apple StoreKit.
  • Payment card details are processed by Apple and are not stored by ClearBox.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • Provide cleanup suggestions and cleanup execution requested by users.
  • Display subscription status and restore purchases.
  • Improve reliability and resolve technical issues.
  • Meet legal, security, and compliance obligations where applicable.

4. Legal Basis (where applicable)

  • Performance of contract/service: delivering requested app functionality.
  • Consent: permission-based access (Photos/Contacts/Calendar).
  • Legitimate interests: app quality assurance and abuse prevention.

5. Permissions and Controls

  • Photos permission for media analysis and selected deletion flow.
  • Contacts permission for duplicate contact detection.
  • Calendar permission for stale event detection.

Permissions can be revoked at any time via iOS Settings. Revocation may disable relevant features.

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Core usage records are stored on-device.
  • Retention period depends on user actions (e.g., cleanup history clear), uninstall, and iOS behavior.
  • When available, local identifiers can be refreshed/reset in app settings.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data sharing is limited to platform-level dependencies required for billing and system APIs.

8. International Data Transfer

Because analysis is primarily on-device, transfer exposure is reduced. If future cloud services are introduced, transfer safeguards will be disclosed before rollout.

9. Security

  • Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are applied to local state and identifiers.
  • Users should secure device passcode, Apple account credentials, and system updates.

10. Children

If you are under the age of majority in your region, use the app under guardian supervision.

11. Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may have rights to:

  • Access information about processing activities.
  • Request correction/deletion where applicable.
  • Withdraw permission consent.
  • Object to specific processing and lodge complaints with supervisory authorities.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this policy for product, legal, or security reasons. Material updates will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, in-app notice.
