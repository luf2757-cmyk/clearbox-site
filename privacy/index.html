Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-25 · Product: ClearBox

ClearBox is an on-device cleanup app for photos, contacts, and calendar data. This policy explains what data is processed, where it is processed, and your choices.

1. Data We Process

  • Photo/video metadata and local identifiers for duplicate/similar/screenshot/large-video cleanup flows.
  • Contacts metadata for duplicate-contact detection.
  • Calendar metadata for stale-event detection.
  • Local app state such as cleanup history, streak counters, and local device identifier.

2. Processing Method

  • Core analysis is performed on-device by default.
  • Photo/contact/calendar content is not uploaded as a prerequisite for basic cleanup features.
  • Purchase and billing are handled by Apple App Store.

3. Permissions

ClearBox requests only required iOS permissions (Photos, Contacts, Calendar). You can revoke permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

4. Data Retention

Local analytics and app-state records are stored on-device until cleared by app actions, app removal, or system cleanup behavior.

5. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards to local state and identifiers. Users should also secure device access and Apple account credentials.

6. Your Rights

You may stop using the app, revoke permissions, clear app state, and manage subscriptions through Apple settings.
