Support Center

ClearBox Support · Subscription, cleanup, and account help

If you experience issues with subscriptions, restore flow, cleanup behavior, permissions, or app stability, use the channels below and include diagnostic details to speed up support.

1. Contact Channels

  • General support: support@clearbox.app
  • Billing/subscription: billing@clearbox.app
  • Privacy requests: privacy@clearbox.app

2. Information to Include

  • App version and iOS version
  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro)
  • User ID shown in app Settings
  • Problem summary and expected behavior
  • Steps to reproduce and frequency
  • Screenshots / screen recordings

3. Subscription FAQ

Purchase succeeded but Pro not unlocked

Use Restore Purchases in Settings. If unresolved, send purchase timestamp and storefront region.

How to cancel auto-renewal?

Manage subscriptions via Apple settings. Cancellation takes effect at end of the current paid period.

How to request a refund?

Refunds are managed by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

4. Cleanup FAQ

"Clean selected" has no visible effect

Check: (1) selected count is not zero, (2) category is unlocked for your plan, (3) Photos write permission is granted.

Scan results look incomplete

Re-run scan after permission refresh. Large media indexing can vary by device state and library sync progress.

Can deleted photos be recovered?

In many cases iOS moves media to "Recently Deleted" first. Final recovery depends on iOS retention rules.

5. Response Time and SLA

Typical initial response: 1–3 business days. Complex billing/platform incidents may require longer investigation.

6. Security Notice

Do not send passwords, full card numbers, verification codes, or private passkeys in support emails.
