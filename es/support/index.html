Please include the following to speed up resolution:

3. Subscription FAQ

Q: Purchase succeeded but Pro not unlocked

A: Open app Settings and use Restore Purchases. If still unresolved, send receipt screenshot + Apple account region.

Q: How do I cancel auto-renewal?

A: Manage via Apple subscription settings. The current paid period remains active until end date.

Q: How do I request a refund?

A: Apple handles refunds at reportaproblem.apple.com.

4. Permission and Cleanup FAQ

Q: Cleanup button has no effect

A: Confirm category entitlement (Pro gating), item selection count, and Photos write permission.

Q: Why are no candidates shown?

A: Re-run scan and verify Photos/Contacts/Calendar permissions are granted.

Q: Can deleted photos be recovered?

A: iOS usually moves deletions to “Recently Deleted” first; retention is controlled by system rules.

5. Response Time

Typical response time is 1–3 business days. Complex billing or platform issues may require additional verification.

6. Security Notice

Never send passwords, full payment card numbers, or private passcodes in support emails.
